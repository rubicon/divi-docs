# Report a Bug

Submit bug reports for Divi 5 through the Elegant Themes member portal.

## Overview

If you encounter a bug in Divi 5, Elegant Themes provides a dedicated reporting system accessible from your account dashboard. Detailed reports with clear reproduction steps help the development team identify and fix issues faster.

For additional reference, see the [official Elegant Themes documentation](https://help.elegantthemes.com/en/articles/9856958).

## How to Report a Bug

1. **Log in** to your [Elegant Themes member portal](https://www.elegantthemes.com/members-area/).
2. Click the **pink chat icon** in the bottom-right corner to open the support chat.
3. Click the **Report Divi 5 Bug** button.
4. Describe the issue with exact steps to reproduce the problem.

## What to Include

A good bug report contains:

- **Steps to reproduce** -- the exact sequence of actions that triggers the bug.
- **Expected behavior** -- what should happen.
- **Actual behavior** -- what happens instead.
- **Environment details** -- Divi version, WordPress version, PHP version, active plugins.
- **Screenshots or recordings** -- visual evidence of the issue.
- **Browser and device** -- which browser and OS you are using.

## What to Expect

The Elegant Themes team will review your report. They will only reach out if they need more details -- you may not receive a confirmation reply. Your report is still logged and tracked internally.

## Before Reporting

To avoid reporting issues that have known fixes:

1. Update to the latest Divi version.
2. Test in Safe Mode (**Divi > Support Center > Safe Mode**) to rule out plugin conflicts.
3. Check the [Elegant Themes help center](https://help.elegantthemes.com/) for known issues.
4. Clear all caches and test in an incognito window.
