# 04-research — What Goes Here

Research artifacts that inform the design, copy, and SEO work.

## Common Files and Subfolders

- **`competitors/`** — Saved pages, screenshots, and notes on competitors. Full competitive analyses typically live in `02-deliverables/`; this folder is for raw source material and follow-up deep dives.
- **`reviews-analysis.md`** — Themes and quotes pulled from the client's customer reviews (Google, Yelp, industry-specific). Often the highest-ROI artifact — client words are typically better copy than anything written from scratch.
- **`seo-keywords.md`** — Target keyword research for the client's market. Feeds page optimization and content strategy.
- **`market-context.md`** — Industry dynamics, trends, regulatory notes, and positioning inputs.

## Priority

Reviews analysis is almost always the highest-value research artifact. If the client has meaningful customer reviews anywhere public, build `reviews-analysis.md` early — it accelerates every downstream copy decision.
